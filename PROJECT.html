Admissions at Mandaluyong College of Science and Technology are now open.

MCST offers FREE COLLEGE EDUCATION as the tuition fee is subsidized by the Mandaluyong LGU.

Please read carefully the step-by-step procedure on how to be admitted at your chosen program. Take note that the ENTIRE ADMISSION PROCESS is fully ONLINE.

ADMISSION PROCESS

STEP 1a: Complete the Admission Form by clicking on bit.ly/MCSTadmission

STEP1b: the last step to completing your admission application process is by uploading 2 Recommendation forms for Step 1a found here: bit.ly/MCSTrecomform2023

STEP 2: Wait for the announcement of results of admission. This will be released via your provided email address in the application form.

Step 3: The email shall contain details regarding the schedule of qualifying examination.

Step 4:Proceed to the designated testing site on your schedule. Please bring 2 pencils, 1 long white folder and 1 ballpen.

Step 5: The announcement of qualifying exam results will be released a day after the test. The results will be notified via the ACTIVE email as indicated on the application form, or via its Official Facebook Page and website. Result of admission is expected to be released on October 5, 2023.

Step 6: Once qualified, please proceed to MCST Administration Building for enrollment along with the indicated requirements during admission and 2 pieces of 2x2 picture with name tag. Enrollment will commence on October 6, 2023.

REMINDER: ADMISSION will last from September 29, 2023 to October 1, 2023 ONLY.

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Application Form: Fully accomplished form bit.ly/MCSTadmission

Photocopy of the Grade 12 Report Card

Photocopy of SHS Diploma

Obtain a certificate of Good Moral Character from the former Adviser, Guidance Counselor, or School Principal.

Mandaluyong Residency Proof:

Submit any ONE of the following:

1.Voter's ID (applicant or parents).

2.Government-issued ID of the applicant, their parents, or legal guardian.

3.Utility bill (e.g., electricity, water) or other proof of billing with Mandaluyong City address

Photocopy of the applicant's NSO/PSA birth certificate.

Recommendations: Two (2) Recommendation Forms filled out by previous educators. Forms are downloadable from bit.ly/MCSTrecomform2023.

2 pieces 2x2 picture with name tag

About Us

Philosophy

Mandaluyong College of Science and Technology advocates the culture of excellence in science and technology that is anchored on the principles of quality instruction, dynamic research and innovation, continuous improvement, public service and nationalism."

Goals

Provide Mandalenyos access to quality higher education.

Support optimum advancement in instruction, technology, research, innovation and resource generation.

Collaborate between and among various educational, technical and professional stakeholders for genuine public service.

Foster institutional effectiveness and efficiency for continuous improvement and total quality management.

Produce graduates who are locally and international competent with high sense of nationalism.

Vision

A college of distinction in Science and Technology committed to produce high caliber and employable graduates.

Mission

To cultivate a culture of excellence in Science and Technology pursuing the improvement of the quality of life of every Mandalenyo to bring about the city’s sustainable development and resiliency towards the nation’s building.

Core Values

Mandaluyong College of Science and Technology Core Values represent the shared beliefs of theMandalenyo. These shared beliefs are captured in the six ideals that define a genuine Mandalenyo. As an institution, MCST upholds the values of:


KAHUSAYAN

NAGLILINGKOD

DISIPLINADO
